The Schedule

We can't wait to see you in Corsica! Here's all the practical info for the weekend.

Saturday, July 4th, 2026

The civil ceremony will take place at the Sisco town hall, located in the hamlet of Munacaghja, at the top of the Sisco valley, at 4:30 PM.

The secular ceremony will then take place at 5:30 PM in the hamlet of Balba.

From 6:30 PM, the party will begin and take place entirely in the hamlet of Balba, on the village square.

🚌 Shuttle service (outbound)

To make it easier to get between the different hamlets, we've arranged a shuttle service that will pick you up from your accommodation at the following times:

  • 3:40 PM for those staying at the Sisco marina – meeting point: in front of the Hôtel U Pozzu (be careful not to confuse it with the Domaine du Pozzu)
  • 3:55 PM for those staying at the Relais de Sisco, the Ferme U San Martinu and Les 5 Arches. Meeting point on the road
  • 4:15 PM for those staying in the hamlets of Balba, Casella, Chiosu. Meeting point on the road

🚌 Shuttle service (return)

During the evening, a shuttle service (minibus) will be available to take you back safely to your accommodation in Sisco.

🚗 Coming by car

You can also use your own car, but please keep the following in mind:

  • The road (D32) from the marina serving all the valley hamlets is very winding, and passing other cars isn't always possible (expect some manoeuvring)
  • Parking in the villages is very limited, and we'd rather not fill Balba (where the party will be) with not-so-decorative cars
  • There may be police checkpoints with breathalyser tests at the Sisco marina on Saturday evening

Sunday, July 5th, 2026

Brunch will start from 11:30 AM at the Domaine du Pozzu, located at the Sisco marina. We'll be by the sea with the option to swim, so don't forget your swimsuits!

No shuttle service needed as a large car park is available.
